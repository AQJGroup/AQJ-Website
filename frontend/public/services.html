Our Services

AQJ Group delivers a full spectrum of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and Digital Construction services — from model setup to digital twin integration — enabling coordination, efficiency, and data-driven project delivery across all stages of design and construction.

BIM Implementation & Management

BIM Execution Planning (BEP)

Comprehensive BEPs tailored to project needs, covering naming conventions, file structures, model exchange formats, and coordination protocols.

BIM Standards & Templates

Development of Revit templates, parameter guidelines, and shared libraries aligned to ISO 19650 and international BIM standards.

Information Management

Establishing data structures, naming conventions, and information delivery specifications to ensure consistent model documentation.

Model Coordination & Clash Management

Our coordination workflows ensure every system and element aligns spatially and technically before construction — reducing rework and improving build quality.

3D Model Federation

Combining multiple discipline models (architecture, structure, MEP) into a federated environment for holistic review and design validation.

Clash Detection & Resolution

Using Navisworks Manage and BIM 360 to run detailed clash checks, generate issue reports, and facilitate multi-discipline coordination meetings.

Model QA & Validation

Implementing custom checklists, naming validation, and element classification reviews to ensure model accuracy and compliance.

4D / 5D Simulation & Visualization

AQJ Group creates time-linked (4D) and cost-integrated (5D) simulations that enable teams to visualize construction sequences, optimize planning, and communicate progress effectively.

Construction Sequencing

Integrating project schedules from Primavera or MS Project with BIM models to simulate build sequences and detect logistical conflicts early.

Cost Integration (5D)

Linking model quantities with cost databases to generate dynamic quantity take-offs and cost analysis in real time.

Rendering & Visualization

High-quality visualizations using Twinmotion, V-Ray and Unreal Engine for realistic project presentations and stakeholder approvals.

COBie & Digital Twin Handover

We deliver structured data handovers and digital twin configurations, ensuring your asset information is usable, maintainable, and ready for operation.

COBie Setup & Validation

Defining COBie schemas, mapping parameters, and validating data exports for FM readiness in alignment with client requirements.

Asset Information Modeling (AIM)

Transitioning project models into operational asset data environments with structured metadata and equipment tagging.

Digital Twin Integration

Connecting BIM models to IoT and facility management platforms for real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance insights.

Training & Support

Our experts provide tailored training programs for project teams and organizations adopting BIM workflows. Sessions include Revit family creation, coordination review techniques, Dynamo scripting, and data management best practices. We also offer long-term support for template governance, automation setup, and QA auditing.
