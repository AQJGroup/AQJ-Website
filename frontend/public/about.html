About AQJ Group

AQJ Group is a multidisciplinary digital construction and BIM consultancy delivering practical, data-driven solutions across design, construction and operations. We specialise in model coordination, federated BIM workflows, COBie-ready deliverables and digital twin implementations that turn design intent into operational value.

What we do

End-to-end BIM delivery, model coordination & clash resolution, 4D/5D simulations, asset data handover and digital twin enablement for the built environment.

Markets

Residential, commercial campuses, healthcare, industrial and infrastructure projects — from concept through fabrication and operations.

Delivery focus

Practical LOD-based models (LOD100–LOD500), COBie-compliant exports, and structured asset data ready for facilities management systems.

Our mission

To enable better-built outcomes by combining engineering discipline with digital workflows — reducing rework, improving coordination and delivering operationally-ready data.

Our vision

A construction industry where models, metadata and process remove ambiguity — where handovers are immediate and projects are delivered faster and safer.

Core services

BIM Implementation & Standards

Template creation, naming conventions, Revit families, and BIM Execution Plans aligned to client requirements and ISO 19650 principles.

Model Coordination & Clash Management

Federation, automated clash detection, prioritized issue registers and clash resolution workshops to deliver coordinated models.

4D / 5D Simulation & Construction Sequencing

Schedule-linked simulations and cost integration to visualise sequencing, detect conflicts and validate buildability.

COBie & Asset Data Handover

Structured asset deliverables (COBie-ready) for FM systems — templates, validation and automated export routines.

Digital Twin & Asset Intelligence

Model-to-dashboard pipelines, sensor integration, metadata modelling and configuration of digital twin platforms.

Training & Support

On-site and remote training, template handover, governance setup and ongoing model QA support.

How we work

  1. Discovery & Standards: Understand client needs, define BIM Execution Plan and standards.
  2. Model Setup & Templates: Families, parameters and federated model architecture.
  3. Coordination Cycles: Iterative clash detection, issues tracking and stakeholder sign-offs.
  4. Validation & Handover: COBie exports, asset register delivery and FM onboarding support.
  5. Continuous Improvement: Lessons learned, automation scripts and training for internal teams.
